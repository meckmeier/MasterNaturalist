# Wild Path - Wisconsin

The Mission: to make it easy for <a href="https://wimasternaturalist.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" title="External link to Master Naturalist site"> <i class="bi bi-arrow-up-right-square "></i> Wisconsin Master Naturalists</a> to find  nature-based organizations. The program asks us to complete advanced training and do volunteer hours annually to renew our designation. So this site was built to help you find these opportunities.

**Activities** = volunteer or training opportunities. Icons are used to help you quickly spot important things. If a training session has a cost there's a little $. Map pictures indicate the general area where activities are. Online activities have a badge. 

**Organizations**  and **Locations** to browse or search for either sponsors or places where activities are happening.

Keep your eye out for the **FILTER** button - that will let you target your view. 

Currently the ability to browse our catalog of organizations and activities is open to the public. If you wish to create Favorite organizations you would need to REGISTER and create a login.


<hr>
### If you are a provider
**Add Organization** -use this menu item to submit a request to add your organization. Once that request is approved, you will receive an email that will let you create a login to manage this organization going forward. A new Organizer submenu will appear and you can use that to access the Organization Management page.

From that page you can add locations, and activities as well as add other users to help you manage the organization going forward.

If you have suggestions or ideas - or find issues or bugs - use the **Feedback** form for that too. It's a great way to report things while it's fresh in your mind.
